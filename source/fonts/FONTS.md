# Caveman Font Stack

All fonts are under **SIL Open Font License 1.1** — free for personal and commercial use.
Download via Google Fonts: click "Download family" to get a ZIP containing the OTF file.

---

## Rye — Titles & Logo
Heavy wood-type display font with rough, chiseled serifs. Feels hand-carved and ancient.

- **Use:** Title screen, logo, large headings
- **Download:** https://fonts.google.com/specimen/Rye

---

## Germania One — UI Headings & Buttons
Bold, chunky, angular display face. Tribal and blunt — pairs well with the Ochre and Bone palette colors.

- **Use:** UI labels, section headers, buttons, HUD elements
- **Download:** https://fonts.google.com/specimen/Germania+One

---

## MedievalSharp — Body & Readable Text
Sharp gothic letterforms that stay legible at smaller sizes. Primitive without being unreadable.

- **Use:** Dialogue, inventory text, tooltips, descriptions
- **Download:** https://fonts.google.com/specimen/MedievalSharp
